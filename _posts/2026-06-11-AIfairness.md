### 1. What is AI fairness?
AI fairness refers a model prefers or underestimates towards a certain subgroup. For example, if a model predicts higher accuracy for female subgroups than that of male; this indicates the model biases to female. This issue can lead to the model ignorance of a certain group, it is very important in sensitive domains like healthcare. 

### 2. The source of bias
There are three common sources of bias: data-centric bias, algorithmic bias, and user bias, corresponding to preprocessing, in-processing, and post-processing stages in the model pipeline.
